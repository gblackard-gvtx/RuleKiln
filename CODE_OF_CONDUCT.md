# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want RuleKiln to be a respectful, practical, and useful open-source project.

We welcome contributors of different backgrounds, skill levels, and perspectives.

## Expected Behavior

Participants are expected to:

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Give and receive feedback professionally.
- Keep discussions focused on the project.
- Be patient with new contributors.
- Disagree without personal attacks.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal insults
- Threats or intimidation
- Trolling or repeated bad-faith arguments
- Publishing private information without permission
- Sexualized or inappropriate content
- Deliberately derailing project discussions

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or block users who violate this Code of Conduct.

Serious or repeated violations may result in a permanent ban from project spaces.

## Reporting

To report a Code of Conduct issue, contact the maintainers privately through GitHub or the project’s published security/contact channel.

Reports will be reviewed as respectfully and confidentially as possible.
